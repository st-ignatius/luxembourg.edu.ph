Welcome to STEM Luxembourg Website

The official website of Grade 11 STEM section Luxembourg in St. Ignatius Cabuyao Campus.

About Our Section

Luxembourg is a STEM section made up of hardworking and talented students. On this page, you'll find our activities, events, subjects, and officers.


Why We Made This Website?

We created the Luxembourg website to show what STEM students can do and to make it easier for us to monitor and manage everything in our section. Even if we’re not coding experts, we wanted to prove that we’re capable—once you log in, you can access the master list, records, honors, achievers, attendance, and more.
